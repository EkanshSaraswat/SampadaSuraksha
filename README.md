SampadaSuraksha is a disaster relief coordination platform designed to manage emergencies like floods, earthquakes, and cyclones. It enables victims to report their location and needs in real time, helping responders act quickly. NGOs, government agencies, and rescue teams can view requests, claim tasks, and update progress through a centralized dashboard. The system also tracks critical resources such as food, medicine, and rescue equipment, ensuring efficient allocation and minimal wastage. By integrating communication, task management, and resource tracking, SampadaSuraksha creates a streamlined, transparent, and data-driven ecosystem that improves coordination, reduces response time, and ultimately helps save lives during disasters.
